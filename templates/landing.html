Questions, answered

What file formats can I upload? ⌄

PDF and DOCX, up to 10MB. Most CVs export well under that.

Does it work for non-technical careers? ⌄

Yes — Claude detects your career type from the CV itself, so nurses, teachers, designers, and accountants all get sections and a theme that fit, not a developer-shaped template.

Can I edit the content after it's generated? ⌄

Every field is click-to-edit, sections and entries drag to reorder, and you can hide any section without deleting it.

Who can see my portfolio? ⌄

Only people you share your link with — and only while your portfolio is set to public. Flip it private any time.
